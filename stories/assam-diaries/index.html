Jorhat, the cultural heart of Assam, has taken the spotlight as India's most trending travel destination for 2026, according to Skyscanner's latest Travel Trends Report. The historic city, famed for its lush tea gardens, rich heritage, and warm hospitality, recorded an impressive 493% surge in travel searches — outpacing international favourites such as Berlin, Langkawi, and Phuket. This remarkable rise highlights a shift in Indian travellers' preferences, with an increasing focus on budget-friendly journeys, immersive cultural experiences, and authentic local adventures. Jorhat's charm lies in its unique blend of history and natural beauty — from the iconic Majuli river island nearby to bustling tea estates and age-old Assamese traditions.
Tourism experts believe this recognition will further place Assam on the global travel map, fostering local economic growth and encouraging infrastructure development. For travellers seeking a destination that offers authenticity over commercialised tourism, Jorhat is poised to be the ultimate choice in 2026.
